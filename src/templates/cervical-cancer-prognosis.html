Cervical cancer prognosis

Predicts likelihood of developing cervical cancer based on lifestyle habits. Used 4 linear Support vector machines to replicate results of 4 medical tests for cervical cancer tests namely the Hinselmann test, Schiller test, Cervical cytology, and Biopsy. Accuracies range from 85-90% on the test set for each model.
