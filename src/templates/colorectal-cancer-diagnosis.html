Colorectal cancer diagnosis through histopathology images.

A convolutional neural network was trained on the colorectal histopathlogy MNIST dataset. The dataset consists of random grayscale crops of 64x64px made from larger high resolution tissue images, with some preprocessing steps applied.

Each patch is classified into 8 categories(Tumor, stroma, complex, lymphoctes, debris, mucosa, adipose, or empty). The algorithm scans the high resolution 5000x5000 tissue sample image and labels each 64x64px square accordingly.
