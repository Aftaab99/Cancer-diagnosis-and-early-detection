Melanoma detection/lesion classification

Trained on the ISIC 2018 skin cancer(melanoma) dataset, the model uses lesion images and meta-data like gender and age to classify a lesion as malignant or benign.

After undersampling the benign samples, and training the model, it achieved a F1 score of 0.83 on the test set.
