Symptoms

Symptoms for the various cancer is shown below.


Breast Cancer

Symptoms for early detection

  • A firm or hard lump in the breast
  • A lumo in the armpit
  • changes in the shape and size of breast
  • changes to the nipple
  • discharge/blood from nipple

Symptoms for late detection

  • Double Vision
  • Muscle weakness
  • Headache
  • Nausea
  • Cough
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Jaundice
  • Loss of appetite
  • Back Pain
  • Weight Loss

Cervical Cancer

Symptoms for early detection

  • Unusual vaginal discharge
  • Abnormal vaginal bleeding
  • Heavier & longer menstrual cycle
  • discomfort while urinating
  • Leg pain

Colorectal Cancer

Symptoms for early detection

  • Blood in stool
  • Change in shape of stool
  • Abdominal cramping and discomfort
  • Prolonged constipation or diarrhea
  • Decrease in appetite
  • Unintended Weight Loss

Skin Cancer

Symptoms for early detection

  • Asymmetry: If you draw a line through the middle of the mole or lesion on your skin, if the two halves are not symmetrical this is a sign that tumor may be malignant
  • Border: Non-malignant tumors will typically have a smooth,regular border.Whereas,the border of an early melanoma will generally be jagged or uneven
  • Color: A tumor of only one color is a good sign because melanomas are known for being a variety of colors, particularly shades of black,brown and tan.They may also be blue,red and other colors
  • Diameter: As a rule of thumb, non-malignant skin cancers are smaller than 1/4 inch in diameter.Melanomas are usually large in diameter,So keep an eye on anything that is large.
  • Evolvong: If the tumor changes in color,elevation size or shape and if any new symptoms arises(bleeding,crusting,itching,...etc) then it could be dangerous
